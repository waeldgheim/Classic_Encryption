What is Playfair Cipher?

The Playfair cipher operates by encrypting pairs of letters rather then individual letters. It employs a 5x5 matrix and uses a keyword to fill in the matrix excluding duplicate letters; the remaining letters of the alphabet are used to fill the matrix.
