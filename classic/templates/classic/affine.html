What is the Affine cipher?

The Affine Cipher is a substitution cipher whose key consists of 2 coefficients a and b constituting the parameters of a mathematical linear function:

General Encryption Formula: E(x)=(ax+b) mod26
General Decryption Formula: D(y)=a−1(y−b) mod26
