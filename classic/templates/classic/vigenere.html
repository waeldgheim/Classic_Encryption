What is Vigenère Cipher?

The Vigenère cipher is based on a shift cipher to which is added the use of a keyword that changes the shift at each step. The user selects a keyword, repeats it to match the message length, and encrypts the plaintext by shifting each letter based on the corresponding letter in the keyword.

General Encryption Formula: Ci = (Pi+Ki) mod 26
General Decryption Formula: Pi = (Ci-Ki+26) mod 26
