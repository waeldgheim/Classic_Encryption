What is the Extended Euclidean Algorithm?

The Extended Euclidean Algorithm finds the modular inverse of 'a' modulo 'm' by calculating two integers 'x' and 'y' such that ax + my = 1. When 'a' and 'm' are coprime, 'x' is the modular inverse of 'a' modulo 'm'.
